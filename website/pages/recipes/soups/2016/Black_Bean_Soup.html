Recipe for 4 Ingredients - 2 cans of black beans - 2 C salsa - 1 onion - 1/4 C cumin - 2 cloves of garlic - salt and pepper to taste Directions Sweat the onions and garlic, then add the cumin, and salsa, and 1/2 can of black beans. Puree the rest of the black beans, and add to the pot. Bring to a boil, taste test and add salt and pepper to taste. Serve with shredded mexican or taco cheese blend, and tortilla chips.
