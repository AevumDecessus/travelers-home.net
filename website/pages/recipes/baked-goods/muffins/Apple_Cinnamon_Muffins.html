pagetitle: Apple Cinnamon Muffins date: 2016-05-18 tags: apple, cinnamon, muffins published: true Beautiful fall flavors and very popular, for very good reason, apple and cinnamon. The most important thing if picking the right apples, everyone may have their own favorites, mine are granny smith. ingredients - 1 1/3 C flour - 1/2 tbs baking soda - 1/5 tsp salt - 1 tbs cinnamon - 3 diced apples - 1/8 C lemon juice - 1/5 C oil - 1/3 C sugar - 1/2 C brown sugar - 6 eggs - 1 tbs vanilla mix dry and wet ingredients sepperatly, then fold the dry into the wet ingredients. fill mufin cups two thirds of the way. Bake them for 30-40 min at 300F
