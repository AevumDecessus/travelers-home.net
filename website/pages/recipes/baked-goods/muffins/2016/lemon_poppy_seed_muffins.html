pagetitle: Lemon Poppy Seed Muffins date: 2016-05-18 tags: lemon, poppy seed, muffins published: true classic combinations are classics for a very good reason, they are good... Recipe for about a dozen muffins ingredients - 2 1/3 C flour - 2 2/3 tbs poppy seeds - 1 1/3 tsp baking soda - 1/3 tsp salt - 3/4 C sugar - 3 eggs - 2/3 C oil - 2/3 C milk - 1 1/3 tsp vanilla - 1 C lemon juice - 1 lemon zest and juice mix dry and wet ingredietns sepperatly, then fold dry ingredients into the wet ingredients. Fill muffin cups 2/3 of the way, bake in oven for 30-40 min at 300F. make a glaze to cover the muffins by mixing lemon juice and powdered sugar till a nice think consistancy to cover them with.
