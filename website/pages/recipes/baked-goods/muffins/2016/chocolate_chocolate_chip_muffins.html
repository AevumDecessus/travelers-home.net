pagetitle: Chocolate Chocolate Chip muffins date: 2016-05-18 tags: chocolate, muffins published: true What is better than chocolate? even more chocolate. Though you could easily replace the chocolate chips of a different flavor. ingredients 3 1/3 C flour 1/5 C cocoa powder 2/3 tbs baking soda 1/3 tsp salt 1/5 C chocolate chips 1/5 C granulated sugar 3/4 C whole milk 1 large eggs, beaten 1/3 cup vegetable oil 2 tsp pure vanilla extract mix dry and wet ingredients sepperatly, fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients. Fill muffin cup two thirds of the way bake for 30-40 min at 300F
