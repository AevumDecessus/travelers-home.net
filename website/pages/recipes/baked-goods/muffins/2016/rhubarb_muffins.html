pagetitle: Heavenly Rhubarb Muffins date: 2016-05-18 tags: rhubarb, muffins published: true Rhubarb is one of my favorite things to bake with. I love the fresh and tart flavor of it, and it looks beatuiful as well. Recipe for about a dozen muffins ingredients - 2 C flour - 2 tsp baking soda - 1/5 tsp salt - 3 C cut rhubarb - 1/3 C olive oil - 1 1/4 C sugar - 1 eggs - 1 C milk mix wet and dry ingredients seperatly, then fold the dry into the wet ingredients. Fill the muffin cupd two thirds of the way. bake in oven for 30-40min at 300F
