pagetitle: Orange Cranberry Muffins date: 2016-05-18 tags: orange, cranberries, muffins published: true Orange and cranberry, one of my new favorite combinations of flavors. I like making it in jam, in cakes, in pies, even in drinks, but this s how it all started. Recipe makes about a dozen muffins ingredients - 2 2/3 C flour - 1tsp baking powder - 1tsp baking soda - 1/5tsp salt - 1C sugar - 2 eggs - 1 1/3C orange juice - 1/3C oil - 2C cranberries directions mix eggs and sugar, then add the oil, and orange juice. Mix all the dry ingredients, then add the cranberries, make sure that the cranberries are covered in the dry ingredients so the berries won't stick together. Fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients. fill mufin cups about two thirds of the way, bake in oven for 30-40 min at 300F
